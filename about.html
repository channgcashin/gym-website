Our Story

Hulk fitness was first established in 1964, aspiring to bring body building interest to the North Carolina area. Today, we still strive to help our customers gain or lose weight and are always striving for perfection when it comes to physique. With multiple unique classes and membership plans, hulk fitness may be the right spot for you!

Location

We are located in downtown Chapel Hill, North Carolina. Feel free to visit us and check out the University of North Carolina's beautiful campus.
